About VAYU

Built with passion, powered by AI

VAYU was born from the vision of creating a truly intelligent voice assistant that runs locally on your machine — respecting your privacy while delivering cutting-edge AI capabilities. Unlike cloud-only assistants, VAYU integrates directly with your operating system, applications, and files to provide a seamless, hands-free computing experience.

Leveraging Google's Gemini AI for natural language understanding and speech processing, VAYU can hold fluid conversations, see your screen, control your computer, and process any file type you throw at it. It's designed for developers, power users, and anyone who wants to be more productive without touching a keyboard.
